CT's Housewares

CT's Housewares is a website you can browse for different housewares products of your choice whether that's by searching or by brand name. You also have the ability to resgister & login to an account & reset your password if you've forgotten it. There is also a profile page with all of the users account details. After you are happy with your cart, you can go to the checkout page & submit a payment, after payment is successful, you can view your order in the 'Order History' section in your Profile.
